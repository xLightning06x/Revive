First Aid Assistance

Real-time guidance using AI and Augmented Reality

How It Works

Our innovative AI-powered tool uses Augmented Reality (AR) to assist you in administering first aid. Simply point your phone's camera at the injured person, and real-time guidance will appear on your screen.

Interactive AR Experience

Follow on-screen prompts and animations to correctly perform life-saving actions like CPR, wound treatment, and more. The AI will analyze the situation and provide tailored instructions based on the injury detected.

Step-by-Step Animations

Animations help ensure proper technique for administering first aid. These include chest compressions, bandaging, and recovery positions, ensuring you can act confidently during emergencies.

Get Started

Launch the AR tool below to begin your first aid assistance journey. Ensure your camera is enabled for the best experience.
